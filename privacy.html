Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

StudyFlash ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our flashcard study application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Password (encrypted)
  • Profile picture (if using Google Sign-In)

Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Study session data (cards reviewed, time spent)
  • Quiz scores and performance metrics
  • Login dates for streak tracking
  • Device type and browser information

User-Generated Content

  • Flashcard decks you create
  • Scanned images (processed locally, not stored on our servers)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the StudyFlash service
  • Track your learning progress and spaced repetition schedule
  • Display leaderboards and achievements
  • Process payments for premium features
  • Send important service updates
  • Improve our application

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, which provides:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage with automatic backups
  • Industry-standard security practices

Scanned images are processed locally on your device using Tesseract.js OCR and are NOT uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication and database
  • Stripe - Payment processing
  • Google Analytics - Usage analytics (optional)

Each service has its own privacy policy governing their data handling practices.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the UK or EU, you have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification - Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure - Request deletion of your data
  • Portability - Export your data
  • Object - Opt out of certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@studyflash.com

6. Children's Privacy

StudyFlash is designed for GCSE students (typically ages 14-16). If you are under 13, please obtain parental consent before using this service. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies and local storage to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences (theme, sound settings)
  • Store offline data for the app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@studyflash.com
